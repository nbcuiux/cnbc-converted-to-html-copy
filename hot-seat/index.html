Summarized Notes

Role

Hot Seat: Assistant Managing Editor
In charge of Hot Seat/Breaking News team

  • Drives traffic on the web, decides where to put the content, decides how important it is
    • When the news breaks:
      • Do we need to get on that?
      • If so, where do we want to put it on the homepage?
    • Story comes from all the different teams
      • staff
      • TV writeups
      • Wires, Partner stories

Goal

  • Get the news out
  • Present the site to get as many viewers as possible
  • Inform the readers about the things they care about

Experience

Worked as Hot Seat Editor for 2 and a half years

Day-to-Day Work

  • Writes the headline for breaking news banner
  • Sends push notification
  • Manages Homepage queue
    • Decides which ones go up, and which to take out
    • Assigns the story that got taken out to a different franchise, e.g. U.S. News page, I-box, Best of
  • Manages Breaking News queue
  • Manages a couple of key boxes
    • e.g. Investing, a.k.a. I-box
    • Best Of: Re-promote the stories that performed well in the past few days
  • Display Watch Live orange banner
  • Update homepage’s upper right corner to promote big guests coming on TV
  • Manages International franchise during International team’s off-hours
    • Noon – 7pm
    • Flip lead stories
    • Add international news that U.S. team covered
  • Goes through 200~300 stories a day
    • Aiming for more volume – double
  • Busy on Earnings days & when big news breaks
    • e.g. Lochte

Team shift

  • 4 Hot Seats, 2~4 hours a day
    • 5:30 AM~9:30 AM, a.k.a. Zero Dark Thirty
    • 9:30 AM~1:30PM
    • 3:30 PM~7:30PM, starts before the market closes
  • Recently changed to 4-people shift, will shift to 3-people shift when someone is on vacation

Task by shift

  • Zero Dark Thirty
    • Redress the page to take out irrelevant international articles
    • Looks for important U.S. News to pull in that international team didn’t
    • Grab big stories from public newspapers, which finish their publication at 6AM
    • Grab important stories on Earnings, which comes out as early as 5:30AM before the bell
    • Any other big stories before the bell
    • Writes up stories herself
    • Cleans up Wire stories
      • Clean up templating
      • Change the main franchise from Wires to relevant topic
      • Hyperlink Person and Place assets, delete the irrelevant links
      • Un-bullhorn the story – all comes in as bullhorned
      • Add art
      • Write better headline
    • Pull in important videos
    • Make sure 2 PRO stories and 2 videos are available on the homepage
    • Work with embeds in each TV shows, and put up Breaking News for big guests, set up artwork, etc.
    • Work with interns and News Associates
      • They help with Partner story grabs and writeups

Communication

  • Production team
    • Asks for Breaking News templates when running low
  • TV embeds
  • Interns & News Associates
  • Copy Desk
    • Asks to prioritize the breaking stories

Prioritization

  • Breaking News, scoop, and exclusives get moved quicker

Often-used Content Type and Features in Toolbelt

Franchise

  • US Top News and Analysis (Homepage) Queue/Franchise
    • 1st story: lead, biggest on the page
    • 2nd~7th stories: on the right side
    • 8th story: almost a second lead
    • Featured: Top stories
    • Secondary: Bigger photos and presentation
  • Breaking News Queue/Franchise
    • Both red banner and push notification on mobile
    • Uses the templates the production teams create in advance
  • Highlights Franchise
    • Don’t Miss film strip on the right rail of the old homepage
    • Lighter, photo-heavier stories
  • International Franchise

Feature

  • Queue tab
    • Gets Breaking News Draft Saved Search
  • Favorites: Saves the frequently used queues
  •  Search
    • Search for Person and Place assets to hyperlink

Pain Points

  • Managing 2 different homepage designs
    • Knows it’s temporary
  • The homepage queue doesn’t show all the assets that go up on the new homepage in a single page
    • Have to go through the queue to select which one to take out, but need to hit Save or Publish every time to go to a different page to not lose the changes
  • Have to juggle with several IDs to replace stories in the homepage queue
    • ID of the story to remove from the queue and add to new franchise
    • ID of the story to add to the queue
    • Harder to do so with the longer homepage and pagination
  • Slack communication is too much
    • Million channels
    • Deletes the app entirely when on break
  • Getting fully templated story published takes too long
    • 15~30 seconds to get a headline up
    • 5 minutes minimum to get fully templated, fully with art, etc. all nice and ready to go
    • Too long to get SEO out
  • Always opting in for ‘Open in the new tab’
    • All the links are being opened in the new tab
  • Assigning Franchise is cumbersome
    • Takes several steps to search and assign
    • The screen has to be centered on Franchise section to drop the asset in
    • Needs to drop at a very specific spot

Wants

  • Homepage queue to show all the assets in the Output List in a single page, not paginated
  • Open links in the new tab by default
  • Anything that would streamline the process of posting the story
  • System recommending the Franchise, Person, Place assets
    • Wire story has these configured when it comes in
  • Better workflow for assigning Franchise
    • There can be only one Franchise, so replace the one that’s already there if a new one is being dragged in – might cause problems for other people
    • Or allow multiple Franchises in there, but bullhorn the most important Franchise
  • Photo workflow to accommodate the new homepage better
    • Currently, only the bullhorned photo shows up on the homepage
    • With the new photo-centric design, would be better to just promote any photo in the article by default
  • First and second lead to consistently show up together on both homepage and mobile

  • Running list in Google Doc
    • Stories that are coming
    • Stories that are in editing process
  • How-to guide
    • How to build a CNBC.com template
    • How to do Earnings

Wire story sources

  •  iNews
    • Specific to business news
    • See things other editors mark as priorities
    • Can’t really search well, so use it as running bulletin
  •  NewsEdge
    • All the wires from partners
    • Search for topics

Communication

  • Slack
    • Has cut down emails
    • Currently mainly communicates with the developers of the new homepage
    • A lot to manage
  • Convo: face all day
    • Slack that TV side uses
    • Gets comments from TV personnels on TV-related stories
  • Everyone that works with Hot Seat sits within yelling distance
  • Phone call
    • TV embeds

Social

  • Dataminr
    • Software that uses algorithms to identify tweets that it thinks might go viral
    • e.g. Kanye, any tweets with the word “explosion” or “school evacuation” or “Did anybody else feel that earthquake?”
    • Just have to figure out when it’s time to tune in and get on a story
    • Found out about the flooding in Baton Rouge on Dataminr: redid our first story with the photos from Dataminr
  • TweetDeck
    • Personally creates list to follow journalists and look out for breaking stories
    • e.g. Separate list for few hours when there was a shooter alert in Bethany College

 

Work Behaviors & Rules

  • Try to give stories as much eyeballs as possible
  • Don’t show the same stories in two different places on the homepage
  • Homepage queues
    • Right now, there are two different homepage designs to manage, so consider both designs when configuring the homepage queue
    • Include a couple of PRO stories, as well as a couple of videos
    • Keep an eye on what’s the 8th story, as it gets great traffic
  • Breaking News Criteria
    • News judgment is part of the Hot Seat’s job
      • Constantly tuning the news judgment with the new leadership
    • Anything affecting the market
    • 5 or more people dying
    • People dying tied to a bigger name
    • e.g. if a plane is landing and it can’t bring its landing gear down, assign someone to watch it and be ready, build template, etc.
  • Have Breaking News Franchise open at all times during Hot Seat shift
  • Package up similar stories or stories that have different take on the same issue together

Miscellaneous

  • Old vs. New homepage
    • Old shows both headline and deck, new only shows the headline
  • Viral stories get viral independent from the homepage configuration
  • The old Breaking News items never get touched, and Breaking News queue just gets longer
  • Embeds: people who are embedded in each television show to write up the big guests, and do the coverage on CNBC exclusives
  • Likes the new homepage
    • Shows more content, and gives longer lives to stories
  • Likes the drag and drop feature of Toolbeltfh
