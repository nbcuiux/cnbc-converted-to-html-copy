Participants

Participants of the second group belong to the teams that are part of the daily editorial workflow. They interact with one another to create accurate and effective news stories that would be on CNBC.com as well as on various social media.

Summarized Notes

Photo Editor

General

  • Everything on the website has a photo
  • Issue: Repetition of the images that get put up on the site
    • Not a 24/7 desk, so people end up using the same images in a row
    • Need to clean up what International and off-hour people have put up
    • Suggested Solution: Flag the photos that have been published in an article within a week – warning message
  • Toolbelt issue
    • Searchability: visually see whathe is looking for, type in any keyword
      • Suggested Solution by Copy Editor: Knowing which photo can be reused without relicensing or going through Photo Desk
      • Suggested Solution bySocial Media Associate Producer: Smart search when there is a typo in the complicated name
        • Photo Editor agrees: slug has to be correct letter by letter
      • Keyword should pull from slug, caption, and source of the image
  • Photo Desk gatekeeps the licenses, controls quality, edits photo
  • Thinks Slack isn’t efficient with so many channels – communication used to be better with the morning meetings

Workflow

  • Everyone comes to them for the photo to promote the content
  • Most important: Hot Seat, News Associates, Editors, Reporters, Strategic Content
  • Pretty independent: Video Producer, International, Prime
  • Site Producer: Troubleshooting
  • Social: collaborate on a lot of things, but relatively independent
    • Photo went up right away in social media might not be the best photos, and he replaces them in the story, but that doesn’t get reflected
    • Suggested Solution: Figure out aworkflow to reflect the change when necessary
    • Suggested Solutionby Social Media Associate Producer: Facebook is controlled and can be modified, but the quickest fix for Twitter would be to delete and post again. Just let the Social team know when there is a photo that’s wrong

Hot Seat Associate Managing Editor

Workflow

  • Interact with all but TVE tagging and Video Editor
  • TV News
    • Not on Slack but on Convo, so have to watch both platforms
  • Copy Editor
    • Too many stories are getting published, so can’t get copy-edited
    • Soft-publishing: getting someone else to read the articles
    • Always have corrections
    • Maybe there is no way to fix this issue because the publication is now lean and fast
    • Better to have the Copy Editor look at later and make corrections rather than have something wrong published
  • Reporter
    • Duplicating effort: There’s Wire or Partner story about a topic, and they write about the same topic
      • Photo Editor has similar issue with the photo requests

News Associate

General

  • Duplication of work
    • e.g. writing food start-up story under Tech, and Special Report team might be covering the same topic for their food vertical
    • Suggested Solution: Centralized filter/flag and control
    • Suggested Solution:See what other groups are working on
  • Toolbelt issue
    • Having to kickpeople out of Breaking News
    • Suggested Solution: Google-docs-like simultaneous editing, e.g. while she’s editing, Copy Editor can copy-edit
      • Copy Editor thinks it’s dangerous – one can override another. Rather, people should be able to edit different fields at the same time
    • Suggested Solution: Track changes feature where the writer accepts other people’s changes
      • Hot Seat Editor likes the idea
  • No way to prioritize different communication channels

Workflow

  • Video Editor: Ask for videos
  • Site Production: Troubleshooting
  • Social:Ask to share on social
  • International
    • Duplication of work
  • Photo Desk
    • Headline changes until the very end, so need to ask for new photos

Social Media

General

  • Mini Badger is very helpful

Workflow

  • Mainly communicate with Hot Seat, Photo Desk, Video Producer, International, Prime
    • Look if there’s anything to add on social
  • Communicate with all but Site Production and Strategic Content
  • International
    • Duplication of efforts: same posts and videos
    • Their standard doesn’t quite meet the US team’s standard, especially their Video Producer
  • Photo Desk
    • Wants to look something up quickly by themselves
    • They help her find the specific photo that she’s looking for
  • TV Reporter
    • They are not on Slack, and it’s hard to communicate with them
    • Emails get lost
    • There is a person in the Social team who is mainly communicating with the team, but she’s moving to another team

Copy Editor

General

  • Everyone who writes will submit for copy editing
  • Wants higher quality video screengrabs to be taken inside Toolbelt

Workflow

  • Almost everyone except for TVE tagging
  • Most interaction: Hot Seat, News Associate, Photo Desk, Editor, Reporter
  • Less direct interaction: Video Editor, Video Producer
    • Find typo, etc. then let them know
    • Coordinating them with putting a video in a story

Most common issues

  • Duplication of efforts: writing about the same story, using the same photos, getting the same photo requests
    • More visibility of other people’s effort
  • Too many channels of communication and many Slack channels
  • Communicating with Reporters are difficult, as they are not on Slack

Prioritization Details & Blue Skies

Copy Editor

Top 3

  • Speed & convenience
    • Whatever can make them work faster
  • Visual search result
  • Google-likesearch of assets

Other

  • Viewing the performance of the stories would be cool, but he would be moved on to the next story. Thinks it’s very important for Hot Seat, so put 5
  • Pop-up for publishing confirmation on Breaking News is annoying
  • Confirmation for publishing on obituaries and embargos would be helpful
  • There is no preview option while someone else is looking at the asset

 

Social Associate Producer

Top 3

  • Easily able to share stories on social media
    • Using external program currently, but would be useful to use a single platform
  • Autosaving
    • Have lost work before
  • Ability to search easily

Blue Sky

  • Searchability, with keywords

Other

  • Ability to edit from mobile would be great, especially with social component

Hot Seat Associate Managing Editor

Top 3

  • Easily rearranging the embedded asset
  • Story autosaving
    • Lots of pain in the Editorial team
  • Google-like search

Other

  • View the performance of the article: using other tools for that right now, but if it works with Omniture and Chartbeat, it would be nice

 

Photo Editor

Top 3

  • Google-like search
    • Pull from various fields, e.g. caption, source, date, slug, etc.
  • Visual display
    • When looking for images, don’t need to click each image
  • Basic photo editing

Blue Sky

  • Image Asset System

Other

  • In general, the issue is that there are too many systems – communication, assets, statistics, etc. Any way to consolidate them would be great
  • Flags on photos – green for no need for licensing, yellow for license by use, red for do not use
  • Something like Assignment Doc existing within CMS would help Photo Desk, too

News Associate

Top 3

  • Story Autosaving
  • Easily rearranging the embedded asset
  • Google-like search
  • Quickly publishing
    • especially Breaking News – publishing after every sentence
  • Efficiently handingoff to the next team
    • Notification within Toolbelt
  • Easily copy and paste from other sources
    • 10 min for Partner grab and 5 min for Wire grab currently, but they would add up

Blue Sky

  • Autosaving
